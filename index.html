Based on A Vision


It is known that the entertainment industry in Nepal, despite being over half a century old, is still in its juvenile stage in terms of the quality and quantity of products it offers. Mr.Pranjal Pokhrel, a singer/songwiter who is a part of this industry along with his peers set on a mission to hopefully help in the evolution of this industry to its prime. Starting from humble beginnings, outsourcing camera and equipments and producing music videos under the banner of other record labels, the journey of the group of these astounding young individuals amounts to the creation of PAR Entertainment, not unlike the industry we are a part of, PAR Entertainment is also in its juvenile stage but our determination to bring about improvement in digital entertainment industry is as adamant as it was on the day we took upon the tast to create this institution.
