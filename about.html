A JOURNEY

A

THROUGH TIME

May, 2020

THE BIG IDEA


During the lockdown in the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, an idea was pitched among Mr. Pranjal Pokhrel and his peers to establish a startup, one which had the potential to become a substantial production studio. This is where PAR Entertainment first spawned in our thoughts.

Jan, 2021

THE BABY STEPS


After the lockdown was lifted, the idea behind the establishment of PAR Entertainent was finally being executed. Well, it took a lot longer than expeted because of the lockdown but we were finally getting there. The team was finally together and the task of getting PAR entertainment up and running truly began.

June, 2021

THE ESTABLISHMENT


On 23 June, 2021, PAR Entertainment was officially registered as a private firm. This opened so many doors inculiding the ability to have our own domain, legitimate social media handles and so on. This date is marked as our official establishment.

MAY, 2022

THE FIRST PRODUCTION


It took us a few months but we finally started on our first ever project: Samaya, a music video based on a love triangle. This signified the first time that we actually started doing what we longed for ever since the inception of PAR Entertainment.

Feb, 2023

THE VALENTINE RELEASE


Even though the production had already ended a couple of months back, we opted to push back the release date of our first official music video so that we coulde release it on the auspicious occasion of valentine. Thus, Feb 14, 2023 made the name of PAR Entertainment known to the public.

THE FUTURE


Although, we cannot be certain about anything. we will try to improve ever more on our work. The motto our organization opted to follow will act as the spine on our operations and all the projects we will put out tin the future.
